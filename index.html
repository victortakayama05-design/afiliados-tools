How to get your API Key

To use CopyForge AI, you need an OpenAI API key (BYOK - Bring Your Own Key model). This ensures you have full control over your data and costs.

  1. Go to platform.openai.com/api-keys.
  2. Log in or create an account.
  3. Add billing credits (Settings > Billing). $5 is enough to generate thousands of texts.
  4. Click the "Create new secret key" button.
  5. Copy the generated key (it starts with sk-...) and paste it at the top of this page.

Your key is saved only in your local browser (localStorage). We do not have access to it and there is no intermediate server. It is 100% secure.
